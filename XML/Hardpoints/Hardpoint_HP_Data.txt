Hardpoint HP Data:

All hardpoint types across RA will have standardized HP values. Below is a list of types and HP values.

Laser Cannons: 30 (set as mass driver hardpoint)
Light Turbolasers: 100 (set as mass driver hardpoint)
Light Quad Turbolasers: 300 
Turbolasers: 400 
Heavy Turbolasers: 500 
Torpedo/Missile Tubes: 400
Light Ion Cannons: 100 
Ion Cannons: 400 
Heavy Ion Cannons: 500 
Beam Weapons: 300
Engines: 1000
Shields: 1000 
Hangars: 800
